About Us

Vanille Pastry is a business that emerged soley out of passion and love for baking and creating products that does not only look good and also taste fantastic. The bakery focuses on handmade cakes, cookies and sweet treats that are prepared fresh using premium quality ingredients. Each dessert is carefully designed to match the customer's theme and preferences, making every order unique and special.

Our passion for baking started with a love for creating desserts that bring people together. From elegant wedding cakes to fun birthday cakes, Vanille Pastry combines creativity and flavor to deliver desserts that customers remember long after the celebration ends.
